About us

SPORT LIONS School is a sport based school run by The Manchester United club members.

Sport lions, co-educational sport based public school, aims at developing the mental and physical potential of each child. It is, and will continue to be, our mission, that each student of our school be a success story in forming a sucsessful sports career.


A spectacular school with the finest sport equipments you have ever seen! We have taught some pupils who are now one of the most skilled sportsmen and women in history.

Our curicullum includes great training, expert coaches, handy knowledge , daily matches and much more!
